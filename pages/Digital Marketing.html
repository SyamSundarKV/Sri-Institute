Digital Marketing Roadmap

Key Concepts in Digital Marketing

Course Objective:
  • Gain a comprehensive understanding of digital marketing strategies and tools.
  • Learn to create and manage online marketing campaigns.
  • Develop skills in SEO, social media marketing, content marketing, and email marketing.
  • Complete hands-on projects to build a professional portfolio.

1
Introduction to Digital Marketing:

  • Overview of digital marketing and its importance.
  • Key concepts and terminology.

2
Search Engine Optimization (SEO):

  • On-page and off-page SEO techniques.
  • Keyword research and optimization.
  • Link building and content strategy.

3
Social Media Marketing:

  • Creating and managing social media campaigns.
  • Using platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter.
  • Analyzing social media metrics and engagement.

4
Content Marketing:

  • Developing a content strategy.
  • Creating blog posts, videos, infographics, and other content.
  • Measuring content performance and ROI.

5
Email Marketing:

  • Building email lists and segments.
  • Designing effective email campaigns.
  • Tracking and analyzing email marketing metrics.

6
Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Advertising:

  • Setting up and managing Google Ads and Facebook Ads.
  • Keyword bidding and campaign optimization.
  • Measuring PPC performance and ROI.

7
Web Analytics:

  • Using tools like Google Analytics and Adobe Analytics.
  • Tracking website traffic and user behavior.
  • Interpreting data to make informed marketing decisions.

8
Project Work:

  • Hands-on projects to apply learned concepts.
  • Developing and executing a digital marketing campaign.
  • Creating a portfolio to showcase your work.
Skills You'll Gain:
  • Proficiency in digital marketing strategies and tools.
  • Ability to create and manage online marketing campaigns.
  • Knowledge of SEO, social media marketing, content marketing, and email marketing.
  • Skills in web analytics and data analysis.
Duration:

Typically ranges from 3 to 4 months, depending on the course intensity and format.

Certification:

Earn a certificate of completion that can be added to your resume or LinkedIn profile.
